Grilled Asparagus

Searching for the best summer side dish that comes together in minutes? Look no further than this easy grilled asparagus thatâ€™s tossed in olive oil, seasoned with salt and pepper, and grilled until perfectly tender.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound of fresh asparagus
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
Directions
  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate
  2. Lightly coat the sparagus spears with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook asparagus on the preheated grill turning often until lightly charred and fork-tendered about 3-5 minutes
